About me

Hi! I’m Andreea. I’m a UX/UI designer with a background in graphic design and psychology. I’m passionate about improving the ways in which we interact with technology by creating simple and intuitive designs.

Creating beautiful, high-quality artworks has been a big part of my recent job. As a hard-working self-starter I am looking to use my visual communication knowledge to wireframe, prototype and design engaging user interfaces for products that are accessible and beneficial to the user. I’m ready to make a difference in an innovative team that aims to bring value into people’s lives and to implement new ideas quickly and successfully, helping to simplify work processes.

A passion for delivering the best experience has led me to teach myself new skills to create unique solutions and content that exceeds expectations. I have gained a variety of knowledge throughout my professional career, and with frontend development and interface skills, I'm capable to communicate effectively within a tech team to create digital products that are easily understood and more enjoyable to use. Take a look at my portfolio: behance.net/andreeacatana and get in touch!"

With almost ten-years experience in an interdisciplinary setting coupled with social science education I am certain I can use my diverse skill set to face with great success various challenges, to deliver in time and in quality, whilst taking into consideration the company needs.

I’m currently available for both freelance and contract work in the Hamburg area or remotely.
